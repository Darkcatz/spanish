Ser or Estar

Ser

Use it to indicate origin, personal traits, physical traits, and professions

Soy de Quito(origin)

los estudiantes son inteligents(personal/physical traits)

la senora Ramirez es maestra(professions)

Estar

Use estar to indicate location and describe conditions

Quito esta en Ecuador(location)

Como estas?(Physical Conditions)

Estoy bien(Physical Conditions)

Estamos contentos(Emotional Conditions)

Estan Enojados(Emotional Conditions)
